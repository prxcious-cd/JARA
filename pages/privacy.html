Privacy Policy

How JARA collects, uses and protects your information.

Last Updated: 1 January 2026 Redeemer's University, Ede, Nigeria

Welcome to JARA ∆ ("JARA", "we", "us" or "our"). We are a campus marketplace platform built for students and businesses at Redeemer's University, Ede, Nigeria.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use JARA, how we use it, and the choices you have. By creating an account or using JARA, you agree to the practices described here.

01

Information We Collect

Information you give us

  • Account details — your full name, email address and password when you sign up.
  • Profile information — your profile photo, school name, course, year of study, account type and a short bio.
  • Listings — product titles, descriptions, prices, photos and your location when you create a listing.
  • Payment proof — transaction references and payment screenshots you upload when subscribing to JARA PRO. We do not collect or store card numbers or bank PINs.
  • Messages — communication you initiate with other users through JARA's contact features.
  • Requests and replies — content you post in the requests and replies sections.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages you visit, searches you make and listings you view within JARA.
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, and language settings.
  • Location — approximate location if you permit it, used to show nearby listings.
  • Cookies and local storage — small data files stored in your browser to keep you logged in and remember your preferences (see Section 5).
02

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate JARA — create and manage your account, display your listings, process your PRO subscription and deliver our services.
  • To personalise your experience — show listings, businesses and requests relevant to your school and interests.
  • To keep JARA safe — detect and prevent fraud, spam, prohibited content and account misuse.
  • To communicate with you — send account verification emails, activity notifications, and platform announcements. You can manage notification preferences in your profile settings.
  • To improve the platform — analyse aggregated usage patterns to understand what features work well and what to build next.
  • To verify PRO payments — our admin team reviews payment screenshots and references solely to confirm your JARA PRO subscription.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third-party advertisers. JARA products are ad-free.

03

Account Security

  • Your password is encrypted and is never stored in plain text.
  • We use Supabase Auth for secure authentication, including email verification and password reset flows.
  • You are responsible for keeping your password confidential. Do not share it with anyone.
  • If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately via the support link in your profile.
  • We recommend using a strong, unique password and keeping your email address up to date.
  • We may temporarily suspend accounts that show signs of suspicious activity to protect the community.
04

How We Share Your Information

We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other JARA users — your public profile, listings and store page are visible to other users within the JARA platform.
  • With our technology providers — we use Supabase for database, authentication and file storage services. Supabase processes data on our behalf and is bound by strict data protection terms.
  • When required by law — we may disclose information if required by a valid legal process, court order or government authority.
  • To protect JARA and its users — we may share information to investigate fraud, enforce our Terms of Service or prevent harm.
05

Cookies & Local Storage

JARA uses browser cookies and local storage for the following purposes:

Type Purpose Can You Opt Out?
Essential Keeps you logged in, stores your session token. No — required for JARA to work.
Preference Remembers your recent searches and notification settings. Yes — clear browser storage.
Analytics Aggregated, anonymous usage statistics to improve JARA. Yes — adjust browser settings.

JARA does not use advertising cookies or sell cookie data to third parties.

06

Your Rights

  • Access — you can view and edit your personal information at any time from your profile settings.
  • Deletion — you may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. We will process your request within 14 days.
  • Correction — if any of your information is inaccurate, you can update it directly in your profile or contact us.
  • Portability — you may request a copy of your personal data in a common format.
  • Withdrawal of consent — where we rely on your consent (e.g. location access), you can withdraw it at any time through your device settings.
07

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile, listings and requests are removed from public view immediately.
  • Aggregated, anonymised usage data may be retained for analytics.
  • Payment records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and financial compliance.
08

Children's Privacy

JARA is designed for university students and campus communities. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you via the JARA Activity tab and update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of JARA after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
